Forecasting Foreign Aid Amounts

Developing countries face challenges in accessing up-to-date information about aid, development, and humanitarian flows. This information is needed to plan and manage resources effectively. There is a large movement which allows different stakeholders to publish data on development cooperation activities. The International Aid Transparency Initiative manages and provides the standard that donors and recipient countries, civil society organizations, and others use to publish and consume data on development cooperation activities.

This is an independent study for the CUNY MSDA and focuses on trying forecast financial foreign aid amounts. The menu to the left has the data workflow (data steps and clean up), maps of foreign financial aid, and the csv query builder for IATI to the most up to date download data.
